Game:Time Work Confidently

Game:Time is the perfect app for staying productive and pulling that all nighter. Whether you're in a boring meeting or class, or trying to beat a deadline, Game:Time will keep you running. Just set the interval you want to be notified and set the alarm. It's as simple as that!

Simple to use

Open the app on your mobile phone, choose how often you want to be notified.

As you work, Game:Time will send you notifications to make sure you're still awake.
